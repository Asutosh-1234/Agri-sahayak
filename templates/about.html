🌱 About Agri-Sahayak

Your Smart Farm Assistant

Agri-Sahayak is a smart crop recommendation tool that helps farmers choose the best crops to grow—based on real farm conditions like soil and weather. We use machine learning to make farming smarter, easier, and more profitable—especially for small and marginal farmers in India.

🎯 What’s Our Goal?

To make precision farming simple and accessible for every farmer—no expensive equipment needed. Just enter your soil and weather data, and we’ll tell you the most suitable crop to grow!

🧠 How It Works

  • Soil Nutrients: Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K)
  • Soil pH Level
  • Weather: Temperature, Humidity, Rainfall

Our trained ML model uses this data and predicts the best crop for your field—instantly!

💻 Technologies We Use

  • Python & scikit-learn for machine learning
  • pandas, numpy for data analysis
  • matplotlib, seaborn, plotly for charts
  • joblib to save models
  • Streamlit for the user-friendly interface

🚀 Key Features

  • Real-time crop recommendation
  • Interactive & mobile-friendly interface
  • Graphs for data insights
  • Educational panel for how and why a crop is suggested

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Empowers smallholders with smart decision-making
  • Reduces overuse of water and fertilizers
  • Increases yield and farm income
  • Aligned with India’s Digital Agriculture Mission

🔮 What’s Next?

  • Multi-language support
  • Yield & fertilizer prediction features
  • Mobile app integration
  • Explainable AI (XAI) for transparent recommendations

This tool follows the vision of Agri-Sahayak—empowering India’s farmers with accessible AI tools, even without costly IoT setups.
