Weather-Based Farming Tips

🌡️ Temperature

Most crops grow best between 20-30°C. Monitor for extreme temperatures that may stress plants.

💧 Humidity

High humidity (>80%) increases disease risk. Ensure good air circulation and consider fungicide application.

🌧️ Rainfall

Track rainfall to optimize irrigation. Most crops need 25-50mm per week during growing season.

💨 Wind

Strong winds (>25 km/h) can damage crops. Consider windbreaks for protection.
